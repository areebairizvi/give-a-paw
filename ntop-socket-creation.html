Latticing¶

Our custom nTop block converts the solid socket shell into a lattice. The point count controls how densely the lattice is sampled across the surface: a low count yields a sparse, open structure that flexes more freely; a high count yields a finer, denser mesh that is stiffer and distributes load over more contact area. Drag the slider to see how the same socket changes as the point count steps from 50 up to 500.
